### 3-Day Family Itinerary in Paris

#### Day 1: Arrival and Relaxation  
**June 2, 2023**  
- **8:00 AM:** Arrive at CDG Airport. Take a taxi or pre-book a shuttle to Hôtel Le Marais (approx. 45 min). Check in and drop off luggage.  
- **10:00 AM:** Explore the Marais district. Walk to **Place des Vosges**, enjoy the park, and visit surrounding art galleries.  
- **12:00 PM:** Lunch at **Les Philosophes** (28 Rue Vieille du Temple).  
- **2:00 PM:** Visit the **Pompidou Center** for modern art (free for under 26). Explore the rooftop for stunning views of Paris.  
- **6:00 PM:** Dinner at **Le Mary Celeste** (1 Rue Commines).  
- **8:00 PM:** Evening stroll along the Seine River to admire nighttime views. Return to hotel for rest.

---

#### Day 2: Historical Paris  
**June 3, 2023**  
- **8:00 AM:** Breakfast at the hotel.  
- **9:30 AM:** Visit the **Louvre Museum** (book tickets online to skip the line).  
- **12:30 PM:** Picnic lunch in the **Tuileries Garden** with baguettes and pastries from **Maison Pichard**.  
- **2:00 PM:** Explore **Île de la Cité** and visit **Notre-Dame Cathedral** (note: access may be limited; stroll the exterior).  
- **3:30 PM:** Visit **Sainte-Chapelle** for its stunning stained glass.  
- **6:00 PM:** Dinner at **Le Relais de l'Entrecôte** (15 Rue de Montpensier).  
- **9:00 PM:** Climb the **Montparnasse Tower** at sunset for breathtaking views.

---

#### Day 3: Iconic Sights  
**June 4, 2023**  
- **8:00 AM:** Breakfast at the hotel.  
- **9:30 AM:** Visit the **Eiffel Tower** (book tickets in advance).  
- **12:00 PM:** Lunch at **Café de l'Homme** with views of the Eiffel Tower.  
- **2:00 PM:** Walk along the **Champs-Élysées** heading towards the **Arc de Triomphe**.  
- **4:00 PM:** Shopping and chocolate shop visits along the Champs-Élysées.  
- **6:00 PM:** Final dinner at **Chez Janou** (2 Rue des Korrigans).  
- **8:00 PM:** Conclude with a **Seine River Cruise** for a final view of the illuminated city.  

---

### Flight Back

### Important Travel Tips  

1. **Learn Basic French Phrases**: A small effort in speaking French can enhance your experience greatly and shows respect for the culture. 

2. **Keep an Eye on Your Belongings**: Stay vigilant in crowded areas to prevent pickpocketing. 

3. **Utilize Public Transport**: Consider using Paris’ Metro for cost-effective and efficient travel around the city.
